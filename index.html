Welcome to FlightDeck

FlightDeck is the unified analytics portal for all Tableau dashboards across the organisation. It brings together reporting from Sales R&M, Product Solutions, and Product Management into a single, searchable interface — so every team can find the right dashboard, request access, and act on insights without friction. Browse by category and subcategory, filter by audience level, and connect directly with the team that owns each view.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is FlightDeck?
FlightDeck is your central hub for all Tableau dashboards across teams. Browse by category and subcategory to find the right view for your needs.
How do I request access to a dashboard?
Click the 'Request Access' button on any dashboard card. This will open a pre-filled email to the relevant analytics team.
How often are dashboards refreshed?
Most dashboards refresh daily. The 'Updated' date on each card reflects the last scheduled refresh cycle.
Who do I contact if a dashboard has errors?
Use the 'Contact' button on the dashboard card to reach the owning team directly, or email soumyasahoo@gmail.com for general support.
Can I request a new dashboard?
Yes — reach out via the Contact button at the bottom of the sidebar, and the analytics team will assess feasibility and timelines.
Why can't I see a preview thumbnail?
Tableau previews require an active Tableau Server session. The portal links directly to the live dashboard when you click on a card.
